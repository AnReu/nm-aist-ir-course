Tentative Schedule

In week 1 and 2, we will introduce basic and more advance topics of Information Retrieval and Text Processing. In week 3 and 4, students have the opportunity to work in groups on a project in a provided setting.

Please refer to the table for a tentative schedule. Each topic will be discussed in a lecture with an accompanying exercise. The exercises include practical tasks written in Python, and blackboard exercises in order to reinforce the content learned in the lectures.

Week 1
June 05 Introduction + Boolean Retrieval
June 06 Pre-Processing
June 07 Vector Space
June 08 Evaluation and Vector Space Optimizations
June 09 Classification
Week 2
June 12 Neural Networks
June 13 Word Embeddings
June 14 Neural Classification
June 15 Transformers
June 16 Back Up + Project Introduction
Week 3 - Week 4
June 19 - 26 Project Work/ Consultation
June 27 Project Presentation
